Stereoh

Social VR Events, 2019-2020

Leading into and situating ourslves within the pandemic, my 2 cofounders and I produced a series of social VR events. They allowed friends to connect in a party context who's soundtracks were provided by local and distant DJs as served up via video walls in the space.

Andrey Radovski and I worked together on the 3D environments, and I handled all things stereoh.one: design and React based development.
