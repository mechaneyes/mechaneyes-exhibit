Social VR Events, 2019-2020

Leading into and situating ourslves within the pandemic, my 2 co-founders and I produced a series of social VR events. The events allowed friends to connect in a party context who's soundtracks were provided by local and distant DJs served up on video walls in the spaces. The events were hosted on the Mozilla Hubs social VR platform.

Andrey Radovski and I collaborated on the 3D environments, and I handled all things stereoh.one: design and React based development.
