So I Do

TBD Festival, 2015

So I Do is a mobile web app I developed and installed at TBD Fest in Sacramento. It's a largely p5.js app that's networked via Web Sockets to other phones running the app at the same time. The app operates as a communal drawing canvas whereby a mark made by one person on one phone is simultaneously displayed on all other phones running the app.

The app was projected nearly 50 feet across, lighting a lounge space at the fetival. By intereacting with the app, users were able to change their visual background and thus the entire mood and atmosphere of the space.
