Monolyth

Monolyth, 2022

A physical installation allowing for the discovery of art and artists

One interacts with the piece using gesture, for which I've leveraged a Leap Motion sensor. The MVP is being prepared for installation in a Fort Greene, Brooklyn storefront. A person walking by will hopefully be attracted enough to take the time to browse through the gallery by waving her hand past the sensor. A QR code driving to the React Native app in development will allow them to explore further.

The piece is being further developed as the vehicle for an ARG-enabled narrative. Multiple Monolyths linked with the app, in addition to desktop+mobile websites, will drive the narrative forward. Inspiration comes from magic realism, film and games such as Year Zero.
