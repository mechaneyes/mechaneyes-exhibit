Better Breaks

Better Breaks, 2022

Mapbox + Audio Visualization

With Better Breaks I built a custom Mapbox GL JS app featuring downtown Sacramento. The elevations of the city's buildings are animated via audio reactivity. Inspiration for the color scheme came directly from Alex Proyas' 1998 movie, Dark City.

I started this project with the intent of including it in Monolyth experiences. Because the Monolyths will be installed throughout the physical world I was looking for a way to facilitate their discovery.

To see Better Breaks in action, enable your browser to use of your computer's mic when following this link.
